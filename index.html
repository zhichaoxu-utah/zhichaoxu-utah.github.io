About [CV]

I am currently a second-year PhD student at School of Computing, University of Utah, advised by Prof. Qingyao Ai. I also work closely with Prof. Yongfeng Zhang and Prof. Mu Zhang.

I am interested in multiple sub-domains of Information Retrieval, including machine learning-motivated IR, i.e. learning-to-rank, learning representations for neural search and recommendation, and Explainable IR

I am also interested in research topics including graph-centric IR, and Natural Language Processing for IR.

Open to Collaboration

I like to collaborate with different people on different projects. If you are also interested into aforementioned research topics, feel free to contact me through email :)
