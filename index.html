About me

I am currently a final year Ph.D. student at Kahlert School of Computing, University of Utah, advised by Prof. Vivek Srikumar and Prof. Bei Wang . I am affliated with Utah NLP and SCI Institute

I am broadly interested in deep learning methods for NLP and Information Retrieval, my previous works involve in-context learning, decoding methods for natural language generation, dense retrieval, recommendation and learning-to-rank.

I am on job market! I am looking for industrial research positions in NLP/IR, reach me at zhichao.xu@utah.edu if you are interested.
